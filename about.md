## 👨‍💻 Who I Am

I'm a Site Reliability Engineer at **Snapp** (Iran's leading ride-hailing platform) with ~3 years of experience in large-scale distributed systems. I'm passionate about infrastructure reliability, observability, and building systems that work.

Beyond my day job, I'm the creator of **Goalixa** — a productivity platform I've been developing to deepen my understanding of modern software architecture, from microservices to GitOps deployments.

---

## 🚀 What I Do

### At Snapp
As an SRE, I work on:
- Working with **OpenShift** clusters for containerized workloads
- Learning and exploring **Ceph** storage infrastructure
- Creating and managing complex alerting rules for system reliability
- Working with message queue technologies (RabbitMQ, Kafka, NATS)
- Ensuring reliability and performance for critical services
- Gaining hands-on experience with large-scale distributed systems

### Personal Projects
**Goalixa** is my learning playground where I experiment with:
- Building complete monitoring & observability stacks (Prometheus, Grafana, Alertmanager)
- Microservices architecture
- API Gateway patterns (BFF)
- Progressive Web Apps (PWA)
- GitOps with ArgoCD
- Event-driven architecture
- And much more...

> **Why the complex architecture?** I know it might seem like overengineering for a personal project, but my main goal is learning. Goalixa is where I practice platform engineering and DevOps concepts hands-on.

## 🎯 Philosophy & Interests

### Learning in Public
I believe in sharing knowledge through experience. Whether it's documenting incident reports, writing about architecture decisions, or sharing monthly progress recaps — I learn by doing and documenting.

### Experimentation Over Perfection
Goalixa isn't perfect, and that's the point. It's a space where I can:
- Try new technologies without production risk
- Make mistakes and learn from them
- Build patterns that I can apply at work
- Explore "overengineered" solutions for educational purposes

### Focus Areas
- **SRE & Reliability**: Building systems that fail gracefully
- **DevOps**: Bridging development and operations
- **Platform Engineering**: Internal developer platforms
- **Observability**: Making systems transparent
