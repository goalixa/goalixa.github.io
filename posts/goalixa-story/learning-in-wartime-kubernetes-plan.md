# Learning in Wartime: Preparing Goalixa Infrastructure

> **Published:** 2026-03-11 | **Section:** Goalixa Story | **Author:** Amirreza Rezaie

While internet is unstable during the war, I’m using the downtime to study hard and plan the next upgrades for Goalixa infrastructure.

## What I’m learning now

- Kubernetes, deeper internals
- CNI with Cilium

- Adopt professional-grade tooling so Goalixa runs reliably at scale

## Why this matters for Goalixa

- Fewer outages and better traffic control
- Stronger security and visibility
- A platform that can keep growing without fragile ops
